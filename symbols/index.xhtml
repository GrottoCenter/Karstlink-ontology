UIS Symbols

This document is an easy-to-read reference to symbols currently recommended by the UIS. This page itself is the standard.

If the standards have been the subject of a vote, the transfer of these standards to this page is in progress. Can you refer to the original documents:
- UIS Basic Cave Mapping Symbols (completed 1999, reviewed 2008)
- UIS Karst Surface Map Symbols (completed 2006)>
- UIS Artificial Cavities Mapping Symbols (completed 2022)
